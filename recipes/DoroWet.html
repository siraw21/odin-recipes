Dorowet Recipe

Dorowet

Dorowet is Ethiopian popular and beloved dish

Here are the main ingredient:

  • Chicken
  • lemon and red onions
  • Spiced butter(niter kibbeh)
  • Garlic and Ginger
  • Eggs and Chicken broth
  • water and tomato paste

Steps

  1. prep the Chicken
  2. cook the onions
  3. Add fat and spices
  4. Simmer the stew
  5. Add eggs
  6. Serve
