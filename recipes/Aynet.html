Aynet

Aynet is popular and traditional Ethiopian dish.

Here are the main ingredient

  • Spicy lentils (miser)
  • Cabbage and potator(Atkilt)
  • Collard Greens(gomen)
  • Garlic and spices
  • Yellow Split peas stew(Alicha kek)

Steps

  1. Make injera
  2. prepare each stew
  3. Serve
