Shiro

Shiro is one of the most popular food in Ethiopian

Here are the main ingredient

  • Shiro flour
  • Barbare flour
  • Oil and salt
  • Garlic and spices

Steps

  1. Saute onions
  2. Add garlic and spices
  3. Add water
  4. Whisk in shiro powder
  5. Simmer and adjust
  6. Serve hor
